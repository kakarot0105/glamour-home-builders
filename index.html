About the Project

Jai Guru Datta Residency is a premium residential project located at Dathanagar, Kanchan Bagh, Hyderabad. Developed by Glamour Home Builders, this GHMC–approved property offers spacious flats with modern amenities and a thoughtfully designed floor plan.

Site Address: P-57/58, Subedar Malla Reddy Marg, Dhatu Nagar, Kanchan Bagh, Hyderabad, Telangana 500058.

Specifications

  • Structure: R.C.C. framed structure; double coat smooth finished cement plastering for inside and outside walls and ceiling.
  • Walls: Good quality red bricks.
  • Doors: Main door made with teak wood frame and designed shutter with brass fittings; internal doors with teak wood frames and flush shutters.
  • Windows: uPVC windows with safety grills.
  • Flooring: Vitrified tiles 600×600 mm in living, dining, bedrooms and kitchen; toilets with anti–skid tiles.
  • Kitchen: Granite platform with stainless steel sink and wall cladding up to window level with glazed tiles.
  • Toilets: Ceramic anti–skid tiles with glazed dadoing up to door height; European and Indian water closets with quality sanitary fittings.
  • Electrical: Copper finolex wiring with modular switches; A/C points, geyser points, sockets, TV and internet points, CCTV points with cameras.
  • Painting: Internal walls finished with Birla wall care putty and paint; external walls with weather proof paint.
  • Water Supply & Sewerage: Concealed plumbing with chromium plated brass Jaguar fittings.
  • Lift: 6 passenger lift.
  • Water Harvesting: Roof water harvesting pits on both terraces.
  • Security: Watchman room, toilet and office room in the parking area as per municipal approval.
